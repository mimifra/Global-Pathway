Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI support work? +

Our AI support system uses advanced natural language processing to understand your questions and provide accurate, instant answers. It can help with general inquiries about programs, visa requirements, application processes, and more. For complex issues, it will connect you to a human specialist.

What kind of questions can I ask the AI assistant? +

You can ask about:

  • Eligibility requirements for programs
  • Document checklists
  • Application deadlines
  • Visa processing times
  • Tuition fees and scholarships
  • Job market information
  • And much more!
Is there a cost for using the AI support? +

No, our AI support is completely free to use. We offer this service to help you get quick answers to your questions anytime, day or night. Only premium services like document review or personalized consultation may have associated fees.

How accurate are the AI's answers? +

Our AI system has been trained on thousands of immigration and education cases with an accuracy rate of over 92%. However, for critical legal or financial matters, we always recommend verifying with one of our human experts through the chat or email support.

What languages does the AI support understand? +

Currently, our AI assistant is fluent in English, Spanish, French, German, and Hindi. We're adding more languages every month to better serve our global community.
